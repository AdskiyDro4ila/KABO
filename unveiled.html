The Collection

Each piece here is a fracture in reality, a glimpse through the cracks of what we take for granted:

  • Echoes of the Void (2024) - A descent into the spaces between thoughts.
  • Fragments of Tomorrow (2023) - Seven meditations on time's collapse.
  • The Unseen Mirror (2023) - Where reflection becomes refraction.
  • Whispers in Static (2022) - Digital dreams, analog nightmares.

A Warning

These works are not meant to be merely read. They are meant to read you. To find the weak points in your certainties. To push against the walls of your understanding.

Some will leave you questioning. Others will leave you changed. None will leave you as you were.

This is the price of seeing. This is the cost of knowing.
