The Curtain Falls. Or Maybe It Never Existed.

You wanted the truth? Here it is: there is none.

I built walls to keep them out. I tore them down. I lost everything and found—nothing. And in that nothing, I saw everything.

I watched fools rise and thinkers fall. I saw the blind leading the blind. I laughed. Then I stopped.

Time is a predator. It devours. It does not wait. If you do not seize it, it will erase you.

I have spent my life peeling away illusions. Here's what I found:

  • Most people are asleep. They talk, they move, they rule—but they do not think.
  • Loss is the only teacher. Comfort keeps you weak. Pain strips you to what is real.
  • Belief is for the desperate. The strong do not believe. They know.

You want to know who I am? Why? Will that change what I have written?

Names mean nothing. Work speaks. Thought breathes. If you understand, you do not need to ask.

Press F. Or don't. The choice was never mine.
