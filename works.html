MY WORKS

What is a work, but a question left unanswered?

To create is not to offer solutions. It is to set fire to what was assumed, to leave behind traces of something that refuses to settle.

What you will find here are not products. Not conclusions. Not neat, finished things to be consumed and forgotten.

These are fractures in the familiar. Open doors that do not promise to lead anywhere safe.

What has been built?

Call them books, writings, thoughts—call them nothing at all. What matters is not what they are, but what they do to you.

  • Some will pull you into the abyss, where meaning dissolves, and only your own strength will bring you back.
  • Some will whisper of things you always suspected but never dared to name.
  • Some will demand to be rejected, for only through resistance does real thought emerge.

What they share is this: they were never meant to comfort. They were never meant to be easy.

How should one approach them?

Not as a follower, not as a collector, but as one who is willing to wrestle. To read is to risk. To understand is to let something change you.

If you seek entertainment, look elsewhere. If you seek a mirror, enter.

The door is open.
