MY WORKS

What is a work, but a question left unanswered?

To create is to set fire to what was assumed, to leave traces that refuse to settle.

What you find here are not products. Not conclusions. Not neat things.

These are fractures in the familiar. Open doors that do not promise safety.

What has been built?

  • Some pull you into the abyss, where meaning dissolves.
  • Some whisper things you suspected but never named.
  • Some demand rejection; only through resistance does thought emerge.

They were never meant to comfort or be easy.

How should one approach them?

Not as a follower or collector, but as one willing to wrestle. To read is to risk.

If you seek entertainment, look elsewhere. If you seek a mirror, enter.

The door is open.
